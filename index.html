Hadi Reisizadeh

Hi, welcome to my page! I am a Postdoctoral Associate at the Optimization for AI Lab, University of Minnesota. I received my Ph.D. and M.Sc. in Electrical and Computer Engineering (with a minor in Computer Science) from the University of Minnesota, Twin Cities, and my B.Sc. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Isfahan University of Technology.

My research develops optimization and information-theoretic principles for safe and efficient machine learning, with a particular focus on large language models. I study how models learn and forget developing rigorous methods grounded in theory that address the core challenges of modern AI. A central theme of my work is to expose the fundamental limits of existing algorithms and propose robust approaches, using optimization and information theory as the core lenses to understand and improve the behavior of foundation models.
